  • An Indian statesman who served three terms as the Prime Minister of India.
  • A member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), he was the first Indian prime minister not of the Indian National Congress to serve a full term in office.
  • He was also noted as a poet and a writer.
  • He was a member of the Indian Parliament for over five decades, having been elected ten times to the Lok Sabha, the lower house, and twice to the Rajya Sabha, the upper house.
  • He successfully handled Nuclear tests, Lahore summit, AIADMK's withdrawal from coalition, Kargil War.
  • In December 2005, Vajpayee announced his retirement from active politics.
  • National honours:
      Bharat Ratna (2015)
      Padma Vibhushan (1992)
  • The administration of Narendra Modi declared in 2014 that Vajpayee's birthday, 25 December, would be marked as Good Governance Day.
